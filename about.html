About Swift Transport

At Swift Transport, we are more than just a transportation company; we are your dedicated partner in every journey. With a steadfast commitment to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we ensure that your travel experience is seamless and enjoyable.

Founded in 1966, Swift Transport has grown to become one of North America's largest full truckload carriers. Our extensive fleet of modern vehicles is operated by professional drivers who prioritize your comfort and safety. Whether you're commuting daily, planning a special trip, or need reliable logistics solutions, we are here to serve you with excellence.

Our Mission: To make travel convenient and enjoyable for every passenger.
Our Vision: To be the most trusted and innovative transport service provider in the region.

Our Values: Integrity, Excellence, Customer-Centricity, Innovation, Sustainability.

Safety & Compliance

Rigorous Driver Vetting

All our drivers undergo thorough background checks, licensing verification, and continuous training to ensure they uphold the highest safety standards.

Proactive Vehicle Maintenance

We perform regular inspections before and after each trip to ensure every vehicle in our fleet meets safety & maintenance benchmarks.

Hours of Service & Fatigue Management

Using smart scheduling and tracking systems, we ensure our drivers take required rest to prevent fatigue-related risks.

Drug & Alcohol Policy

Zero-tolerance policy: tests are conducted on hiring, randomly, and after incidents to ensure driver sobriety and public safety.

Real-Time Monitoring & Telematics

GPS, behavior analytics, and telematics help us monitor driving patterns like harsh braking, speeding, and route adherence to continuously improve.

Incident Response & Reporting

We maintain clear procedures for reporting and investigating incidents and near-misses to identify root causes and prevent recurrence.
